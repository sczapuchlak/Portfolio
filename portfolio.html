My name is Stephanie Zapuchlak and I am a Web Developer and Designer. I have a passion for making beautiful, cohesive sites with intuitive interfaces. Not only do I enjoy the design process, but coding has become one of my favorite pastimes. I am based out of Minneapolis, Minnesota , but like a true Wisconsinite I live every day with compassion and a warm soul ( despite the icy cold weather *le sigh* ). No matter the distance or where I end up, my heart still lies in the Wisconsin traditions of bubbly beer, great cheese, and interesting conversation.

From a young age, I have always loved the elements of design. For Christmas, I'd ask my parents for label makers, gel pens, and font books. I would sit for hours looking at pretty fonts and designing works of art (more like kid scribbles) in KidPix and PrintShop. Fast forward 20 years, I started working as a hairstylist, where I could use that creative freedom to create lovely works of hair art. But something was still missing. In my spare time in between clients, I'd hop on the computer to touch up client before/after photos and work on the company blog. The more I sat behind the computer and designed, the more I felt at home. I kept ignoring that little whisper in the back of my head to do something in the tech world, until I uprooted to Minneapolis and started fresh. I went back to school in 2015 for my A.A.S in software development and am in the midst of a Bachelors of Science for Internet and Web Development. Every single day I wake up to experience more of what I love and couldn't be more excited to grow professionally and creatively.
